Sexuality by Design Curriculum

Sex and sexuality. Culture bombards teens with messages like “anything goes” and “it’s not a big deal.” But God tells us it is a big deal—a gift to be valued, respected, and governed by choices that honor Him, others, and ourselves. Sexuality by Design is a mixed-gender, highly-customizable curriculum to equip teens to steward their sexuality as they:

  • ASSESS what current culture says about sex,
  • ACKNOWLEDGE what God says in Scripture, reinforced by biological and behavioral science, and
  • ALIGN themselves with His design through activities, discussions, and realistic strategies.
